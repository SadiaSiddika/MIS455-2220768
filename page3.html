After harvesting, broccoli is quickly cooled using ice or hydro-cooling to preserve its freshness. It is then stored in cold storage at 0-4°C (32-39°F) with high humidity to prevent wilting. Ventilated crates or modified atmosphere packaging help maintain quality during storage and transport. This ensures that broccoli remains fresh until it reaches markets or consumers.
